About

A life devoted to healing

Gena M. Rotas is a Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker with over three decades of experience in psychotherapy, life coaching, and mindfulness-based practice. Through her private practice, Rotas & Associates, she serves individuals, couples, adolescents, families, and organizations across Massachusetts and Colorado.

Gena's approach blends evidence-based clinical methods with heart-centered, holistic techniques — including HeartMath, Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT/Tapping), and mBraining — to address trauma, addiction, anxiety, self-worth, and the universal human desire to simply feel better in one's own skin.

She has delivered training to institutions including MassMutual Financial and Bay State Medical Center, and has trained teachers and staff across Massachusetts and Connecticut school districts on mindfulness and social-emotional wellness.

At the core of everything Gena does is a simple conviction: happiness is a choice — and with the right tools, it's one anyone can make.
